LazrPro++

LazrPro++ is the latest fiber laser cutting head. Widely used by our company laser machines with features: attractive appearance, novel structure, easy operation, accurate focus, reasonable airflow combination, etc.

Summarize

Prior applicable for low&medium power solid and gas laser cutting machines.

Cutting head is combined with:

BH module, Collimating lens module, mounting plate, auto-following amplifier, focus lens module, Focus adjustment module, protective mirror module, sensor module.

Optical fiber put in and take out method

QBH plug put in sequence.
1. Take off the dust cover on the chuck.
2. Aligning the red point from plug to the QBH chuck, then insert into the bottom.
3. According to the mark on QBH chuck，turn left the screw nut(45°), then pull up 1.25mm, then turn left (30°), finally, finish the Fixed lock program.

QBH plug take out sequence.
1.QBH plug pull out sequence is contrary to put in. Turn right (30°), push off 1.25mm, turn right(45°), finally, can take the plug out of the QBH chuck.
2.Be attention of the environment during pull out process, make sure no dust inside.

QCS plug put in and take out method.
This product can also applied for QCS fiber plug. The operation sequence as below:
1. Take off the dust cover on the chuck.
2. Anti-clockwise direction loosen the nut of chuck.
3. Put the QCS plug inside and tightening the nut clockwise.
Sequences are in verse for putting in and taking out.
Cover up the dust cap after taking QCS plug out, To prevent dust intrusion.

Focus instruction

There is a focus centring module on the top of protective mirror. And 4 centring adjust screw all around the module.

Advantages: Rigid positioning, focus is stable after locked the centring adjust screw.

Centring method: if need move to the left, loosen the screw left side and then tighten the screw right side. Vise verse.

Finish the centring work, fix these two screws and then locked them.

The method are the same if want to move the module front and back.

Focus lens adjusting method

Focus lens adjusting organization realize focus on Z axis with high-precision screw which have perfect self-locking and fine focusing performance.

Focusing module has adjusting hand wheel with 50 equal scale, every scale, focus lens will go up or down 0.02mm, every round, the lengthways pole will go up or down 1mm. The lens can move vertical 10mm. There are scales 0,+5,-5,0 which near the nozzle, +5 means the focus retract into the nozzle the most. -5 means out the most. There is an insurance screw on the side of focusing module. You can use it to adjust the mechanical clearance if the cutting head keep long-term work.

Adjustment of Collimating Mirror

he focal distance are different from different manufactures. There is a focus adjusting module under Collimating lens seat.

There are total 10 scales on the focus adjusting wheel: 0-center of mirror,+5-top, -5-bottom. If need to adjust light beam, unlock screw, adjust, then lock.

Remark: location of mirror are adjusted well before machine exit warehouse. Users no need to adjust more the time.

Maintenance of focus lens

Focus lens under the adjusting module, need to do maintenance periodically. It’s better 1-2 months per time under clean environment.

Taking out of the focus lens case

1. Unscrew four screws, take off module in bottom.

2. Take out the focus lens case with Wire ring spanner. Attention: can only take it our if spanner bayonet align to the bayonet on mirror case.

3. After taking out the case, use wire ring spanner screw wire ring and take out the plastic spacer and focus lens.

Cleaning of focus lens

Tools: dust proof glove, Long fiber cotton wool, isopropyl alcohol, rubber blower.

Cleaning method:
Take on glove, hold the mirror, use alcoholled cotton wool clean the mirror. Cleaning method: from down to up, or from left to right, clean the mirror lightly unidirection. Please do not clean the mirror back and force which can lead to secondary pollution. Need to blow the surface of mirror after cleaning.

Installation of focus lens case

The sequence for taking out and put inside of the lens case are opposite. Put focus lens, plastic spacer, wire ring inside of the focus lens case, then fix wire ring use spanner, finally put the case into focal adjusting module.

Maintenance of the protective mirror

Protective mirror is under the centring module, easy polluted, should be cleaned every week.

Taking out of the protective mirror case

Hole the handle of the protective mirror case, pull out slightly, seal the drawer with gummed paper to proof dust inside.

Cleaning of protective mirror

Screw out the wire ring with spanner, take out the mirror, and clean it with the same method of focus lens cleaning.

Installation of protective mirror case

Protective mirror is plane mirror, can be put inside of the case directly. Sequence is opposite to the way taking out. Press the handle on the drawer and locked it. Then put inside, finish.

Replacement of quick-wear part

The nozzle under the cutting head is easy damaged. User can contra-rotate the nozzle easily. After replace the new nozzle, capacitance calibration work is needed.

If the ceramic body is broken, need to take off the nozzle pressure ring, take it out, and replace a new one. Attention, need to align the location hole on the sensor seat to the gauge pile, then tightening the pressure ring. Otherwise, the sensor seat can be damaged.
